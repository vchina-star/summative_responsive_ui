About

The student finance tracker helps you see where your money actually goes. So you can make better financial decisions, build financial discipline and still find room to save. Add your daily transactions,sort them into categories, set spending caps and quickly search your full history. Everything runs in your browser, works offline and is built to be accessible.

Features

  • Track transactions with categories and dates
  • Dashboard with spending stats and trends
  • Regex-powered search with match highlighting
  • Budget cap with live overage alerts
  • Multi-currency support with manual rates
  • Import/export data as JSON
  • Fully keyboard accessible
